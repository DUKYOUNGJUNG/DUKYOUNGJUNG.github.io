NanoCity — Help 🏝️

How to play

NanoCity is a game where you grow a tiny island town right on your home screen widget. Check in now and then and enjoy watching it grow stage by stage. We hope this guide helps you get started.

1. Getting started

NanoCity is a tiny island town that grows on your home screen widget. The town grows a little each time the widget refreshes.

⚠️ Adding the widget is required.
Your town only grows while the widget is on your home screen, because growth happens as the widget refreshes. Without it, progress stops.

How to add — Long-press the home screen → Add Widget → NanoCity → Add

Widget size: Small and Large widgets are supported. The Large widget shows the town better, which makes growing it more fun.

2. Growing your town

  • Stage: As your town grows it levels up and gets bigger.
  • Population / cap: Building houses adds residents. Once the cap is full, it stops rising.
  • Dissatisfaction: If houses, wells, or shops are lacking, residents get unhappy. Too high and the town can drop a stage (demotion). It eases off over time.
  • Coins 🪙 / per hour / Collect: Your town produces coins each hour. Tap "Collect" on the home tab to gather them.
  • Decisions: Now and then the widget or app shows a "What should we build?" choice. Tap to pick, and the town grows that way.
🪙 How to earn coins
Coins come from ① resident requests and ② the Worksite (see Plaza facilities below). When you start a new town you get a free starter kit — 1 logging camp + 2 workers — so income flows right away.
🧭 Choosing your town's path
At certain stages you choose "What kind of place is this town?" (market street, park town, metropolis, production town, and so on). This choice becomes your town's identity.

Completion & Collection

Grow to the final stage and your town is complete. A god evaluates it (identity × rarity), and finished towns join your Collection and earn medals. Then you start a new town.

👑 Progress to the god's evaluation
The crown bar in the Goal box on the home tab shows how close you are to completion. It fills as you level up and drops if you're demoted. Hold at the final stage for a while and the bar fills, opening the god's evaluation.

The widget shows a small crown and progress % for a quick glance.

3. Screens (tabs)

  • Town (Home): View your town, collect coins, manage widget features, rename your town (pencil), notices & sharing.
  • Plaza: A hub of facilities — requests, shops, worksite, exchange, library, statue (see section 4).
  • Collection: Every town you've completed.
  • Settings: Music, sound effects, haptics, NanoCity Plus, Help, app info.

4. Plaza facilities

The Plaza tab has several buildings. Tap to enter each.

  • Resident Requests: Craft what residents ask for (pay a material cost); after some time you collect a coin reward.
  • Feature Shop: Unlock the "Widget features" below with coins.
  • General Store: Buy decorations like lamps, benches, flower beds, and lighthouses with coins.
  • Workshop: Change building designs (skins) — log cabin, brick house, open-air café, and more.
  • Worksite (Labor Office): Buy production facilities (logging camp, quarry, mine) and hire workers to staff them — they produce coins per hour. Higher stages allow more facilities and workers.
  • Medal Shop: Spend medals (earned by completing towns) on rare decorations (bronze figurine, golden statue, and so on).
  • Exchange: Trade spare medals for coins. The rate changes daily (shown as a chart), with a daily exchange limit.
  • Library: Opens once you unlock the Wordbook feature (see "Widget features — Wordbook").
  • Statue: Place a medal statue in the center of the plaza.
🏅 Unlocked features, medal purchases, and word packs you buy stay permanently on your account — they carry over to new towns. (Decorations, skins, and worksite facilities are set up fresh each town.)

5. Widget features

Once unlocked in the Feature Shop, these appear in your town and widget. You can tap them right on the widget to interact.

Only one "always-on" display at a time — choose among Airship / Photo balloon / To-do board.
The others (UFO, Animal, Water Fairy, Carrier Pigeon, Stargazer, Wordbook) can be toggled freely.

🕰️ Airship

An airship shows the live time or a D-day (D-N to your target date). Switch with the Clock/D-day toggle in widget features; set a target date for D-day.

📷 Photo balloon

Shows one photo from your box as a balloon every 30 minutes. Tap the balloon to view it larger.

🦉 Owl Mailcarrier (To-do board)

Connect Reminders to show today's to-do count in your town.

📨 Carrier Pigeon (Schedule)

A pigeon delivers your next calendar event — see the date, time, and title at a glance in your town.

🔭 Stargazer (Sky events)

Notifies you of tonight's sky events like a full moon or meteor shower. Tap for details and the direction to look.

📖 Wordbook (Word of the day)

Around lunchtime, a word of the day appears on the widget. Tap to see meanings, examples, and pronunciation playback.

  • At the Library (Plaza), buy themed word packs (animals, food, travel, and more) with coins, and toggle them on/off. Enabled packs each contribute a word a day.
  • My Words: Add your own words and meanings to build a personal pack.
  • Language: Choose the word language and meaning language in the Wordbook settings on the town screen.

🛸 UFO

Delivers today's space photo (NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day) each evening.

🐾 Animal Courier

Delivers today's animal friend each morning.

💧 Water Fairy

At hydration times a water fairy briefly appears as a reminder. Tap to log a cup; set your daily cup goal and reminder hours.

6. NanoCity Plus (subscription)

A monthly subscription unlocks these premium visuals.

  • Weather effects: Rain, snow, and fog appear in your town.
  • Premium weather screen: Hourly, weekly, and air-quality info.
  • Golden skin set: Dress your feature buildings in gold.
If you cancel, premium-only visuals return to default. Your town progress is kept. 😊
